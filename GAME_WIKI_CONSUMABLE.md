## SYSTEM OVERVIEW

- **29 total consumables** crafted via Cooking (15) and Biochemistry (14)
- Each hero carries a **Combat Bag** with 1-6 consumable slots
- Consumables are used during combat and consumed on use (quantity decreases)
- Each consumable has a **cooldown** preventing spamming
- **Buff consumables** provide timed stat bonuses (duration in seconds)
- **Instant consumables** provide immediate healing (HP, SP, or both)
- A hero cannot equip the same consumable in multiple slots
- Consumables are crafted from gathered resources and can be traded on the Marketplace

## COMBAT BAG SLOT PROGRESSION

Each hero starts with **1 consumable slot** and unlocks additional slots as they level up, for a maximum of **6 slots**.

| Slot # | Unlock Level | Cumulative Slots |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| 1 | 1 (base) | 1 |
| 2 | 15 | 2 |
| 3 | 30 | 3 |
| 4 | 45 | 4 |
| 5 | 60 | 5 |
| 6 | 80 | 6 |

**How it works:**
- Go to the Heroes tab and select a hero
- The Consumable Bag section shows all 6 slots
- Locked slots display the required level to unlock
- Click an unlocked slot to equip/swap a consumable
- You must have the consumable in your resource inventory to equip it
- The same consumable cannot be equipped in multiple slots on the same hero

## BUFF VS INSTANT CONSUMABLES

### Buff Consumables (Timed)
- Provide stat bonuses for a set **duration** (seconds)
- Multiple different buffs can be active simultaneously
- Effects listed as percentage (%) or flat value
- Duration ranges from 2 to 10 minutes
- Cooldowns prevent re-application before the buff expires
- **All Cooking consumables** are buff-type
- Some Biochemistry chemicals are buff-type

### Instant Consumables
- Provide **immediate** healing (HP, SP, or both)
- Duration = 0 (instant effect)
- Cooldowns still apply between uses
- **Stimpak**, potions, and Full Restore are instant-type
- Cleansing Agent and Antidote are technically buff-type (grant status resistance)
