# Consumables

There are **29 consumables** across two production skills: **Cooking** (15) and **Biochemistry** (14). Heroes can equip consumables based on their level (max 6 slots at level 80+). Each equipped consumable is consumed **once per fight** (1 unit deducted from inventory). When a consumable runs out of stock, it is automatically unequipped.

## Consumable Slot Unlocks
| Hero Level | Slots |
|------------|-------|
| 1 | 1 |
| 15 | 2 |
| 30 | 3 |
| 45 | 4 |
| 60 | 5 |
| 80 | 6 (max) |
