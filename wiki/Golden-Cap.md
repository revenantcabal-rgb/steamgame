# Golden Cap (Premium Pass)

The Golden Cap is Wasteland Survivor's optional premium subscription that provides quality-of-life bonuses.

---

## Pricing

| Duration | Price | Savings |
|----------|-------|---------|
| 7 days | $5.99 | — |
| 15 days | $9.99 | 22% |
| 30 days | $16.99 | 34% |
| 90 days | $39.99 | 48% |
| 365 days | $119.99 | 62% |

**Max stack**: 2 years (730 days). Duration stacks if purchased multiple times.

---

## Benefits

| Benefit | Free | Golden Cap |
|---------|------|------------|
| XP Multiplier | 1.0× | 1.2× (+20%) |
| Worker Respawn | 3 min | 2 min 15 sec (0.75×) |
| Hero Recruit Cost | 1.0× | 0.85× (15% discount) |
| Enemy Wave Scaling | 1.0× | 0.75× (25% slower growth) |
| Resource Drop Chance | +0% | +20% |
| Loot Tracker History | 12 hours | 24 hours |
| Worker Auto-Reassign | No | Yes |

### Detailed Benefits

- **+20% XP**: Applies to ALL activities — combat, gathering, production, worker XP
- **Faster Worker Respawn**: Dead workers return 25% faster (2:15 vs 3:00)
- **Hero Recruit Discount**: 15% cheaper recruitment costs
- **Slower Enemy Scaling**: Wave multiplier grows 25% slower in combat zones
- **+20% Drop Chance**: All resource drops have 20% higher chance
- **Extended Loot Tracker**: See 24 hours of drop history instead of 12
- **Worker Auto-Reassign**: Workers automatically return to their previous assignment after respawning
