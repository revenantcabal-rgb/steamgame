## Special Resource

### Icqor Chess Piece
- **Source**: Boss drops in combat zones (15%–80% chance by zone) and rare gathering drops
- **Gathering drop chance**: T3 activities 0.5%, T2 0.3%, T1 0.1%
- **Use**: Currency for unlocking Starlight Constellation nodes
