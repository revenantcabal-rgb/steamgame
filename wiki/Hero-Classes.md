# Hero Classes & Categories

There are **16 hero classes** organized into **5 categories**. Each hero starts with **10 in all stats**, with their primary stat boosted to **13** based on combat style (STR for melee, DEX for ranged, INT for demolitions). Support heroes always get CON 13 regardless of combat style.

Heroes gain **6 stat points per level** and have a **level cap of 100**.

**Specialist heroes** (Artisan category) deal **80% combat damage** but have unique gathering/production bonuses.

## Ability Slot Unlocks (via RES stat)
| RES Level | Ability Slots |
|-----------|---------------|
| Base | 1 |
| RES ≥ 30 | 2 |
| RES ≥ 60 | 3 |
| RES ≥ 90 | 4 |

| Hero Level | Consumable Slots |
|------------|------------------|
| Base | 1 |
| Level 15 | 2 |
| Level 30 | 3 |
| Level 45 | 4 |
| Level 60 | 5 |
| Level 80 | 6 (max) |

## Decree Slot
- Unlocked at **RES ≥ 50**
- 1 slot for Purple (Warband Decree) tomes
- Only 1 decree per party
