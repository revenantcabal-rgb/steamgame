## Game Overview

**Wasteland Survivor** is a browser-based idle RPG set in a post-apocalyptic world. Players recruit heroes, gather resources, craft equipment, fight through combat zones, and progress through a gated story system that unlocks new features.

### Core Gameplay Loop
1. **Gather** resources using skills and workers
2. **Craft** gear, consumables, and tools
3. **Recruit** heroes and allocate stats
4. **Deploy** heroes to combat zones for idle fighting
5. **Progress** through story chapters to unlock new systems
6. **Trade** on the marketplace
7. **Invest** Icqor Chess Pieces into the Starlight constellation
