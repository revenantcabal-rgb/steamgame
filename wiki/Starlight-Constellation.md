# Starlight Constellation

The Starlight system is an **account-level passive bonus tree** with **105 nodes** across **7 paths**. Nodes are unlocked by spending **Icqor Chess Pieces** (dropped by bosses and rarely from gathering).

Starlight bonuses apply to **ALL heroes** on the account.

---

## Paths Overview

| Path | Color | Focus | Primary Stat |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| Warfare | Red | Melee/STR | Crit damage, armor pen, lifesteal |
| Marksmanship | Green | Ranged/DEX | Crit damage, crit chance, evasion |
| Demolitions | Blue | Blast/INT | DoT effects, SP scaling, SP regen |
| Fortification | Orange | Tank/CON | Defense, damage reduction, block chance |
| Spirit | Purple | Ability/RES | SP scaling, SP cost reduction, crit damage |
| Survival | Teal | Utility/LUK | Drop chance, evasion, gathering bonuses |
| Industry | Gold | Production | Production speed, XP bonus, double output |

---

## Structure

Each path has **15 nodes** across **5 rings** (3 nodes per ring):

| Ring | Nodes | Cost per Node | Total per Path |
|------|-------|---------------|----------------|
| Ring 1 | 3 | 1 Icqor | 3 |
| Ring 2 | 3 | 2 Icqor | 6 |
| Ring 3 | 3 | 4 Icqor | 12 |
| Ring 4 | 3 | 7 Icqor | 21 |
| Ring 5 (Capstone) | 3 | 10 Icqor | 30 |
| **Path Total** | **15** | — | **72 Icqor** |
| **All 7 Paths** | **105** | — | **504 Icqor** |

### Progression Rules
- Must unlock at least 1 node from the previous ring to access the next ring
- Ring 5 (Capstone) nodes have **2 effects** each
- Bonuses with `_pct` suffix are percentage increases; others are flat
