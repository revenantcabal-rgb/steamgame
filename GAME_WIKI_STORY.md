## SYSTEM OVERVIEW

- **5 chapters**, each with **7 tasks** (parts), for a total of **35 tasks**
- Tasks must be completed sequentially within each chapter
- Each chapter unlocks a major game feature upon completion
- Tasks track progress automatically (gathering, crafting, combat, etc.)
- Part rewards are granted immediately on completion
- Chapter completion rewards are granted after the final part
- Story progress is saved with the character slot

## STARTER HERO CHOICE

When a new character slot is created, the player must choose a **starter hero** before entering the game. The starter hero is free and can be any of the 16 available classes:

**Combat Classes (12):**
- Skirmisher: Blade Dancer, Sharpshooter, Sapper
- Control: Warden, Trapper, Bombardier
- Support: Guardian, Field Medic, Chemist
- Assault: Berserker, Deadeye, Demolisher

**Specialist Classes (4):**
- Artisan: Scavenger, Ranger, Prospector, Artificer

The starter hero is always free. Additional heroes require completing Story Chapter 3 to unlock recruitment, and cost Wasteland Credits.

---

## HERO RECRUITMENT COSTS

After completing Story Chapter 3, players can recruit additional heroes at escalating costs:

| Hero # | Cost |
|--------|------|
| 1st (Starter) | Free |
| 2nd | 750 WC |
| 3rd | 1,000 WC |
| 4th | 1,250 WC |
| 5th | 1,500 WC |
| Nth | 500 x (1 + N x 0.5) WC |

Formula: `cost = floor(500 * (1 + currentHeroCount * 0.5))`
